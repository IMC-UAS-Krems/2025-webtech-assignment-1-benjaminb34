About Me

My name is Benjamin. I am a student at IMC Krems University, where I study Informatics. I come from Hungary and often travel back and forth between Austria and Hungary by train. I actually created part of this webpage on the train itself. Not the most comfortable place to work, but I didn’t want to waste time!

I used to play ice hockey for several years. I sometimes miss it, but it’s difficult to keep up with the sport while studying at university. Since I stopped playing, I’ve been looking for other activities to do. One of my dreams is to climb mountains someday. I’ve never done it before, but I really want to try.

I have a lot of different interests. I enjoy computer science, physics, mathematics, history, and philosophy. My coolest hobby is astrophotography. I used to take pictures of the planets and the Moon with my own telescope. Nowadays, I do more computer-related projects because I have less time, and the planets aren’t visible from my balcony right now.

And one very important fact about me, I have a French Bulldog.
